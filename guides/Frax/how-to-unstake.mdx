## Step 1: Navigate to the Staking Platform

Visit our staking platform at [frax.dogelonmars.com](https://frax.dogelonmars.com/).

![Fraxhome Pn](/images/HowToStake/fraxhome.png)

## Step 2: Connect your Wallet

Click the 'Connect Wallet' button and follow the prompts to connect your wallet.

![Fraxconnect Pn](/images/HowToStake/fraxconnect.png)

## Step 3: Choose your Wallet

Select your preferred wallet from the options provided.

![Fraxchoosewallet Pn](/images/HowToStake/fraxchoosewallet.png)

## Step 4: Access Your Staked Assets

Find the "Current Stake" tab that displays your staked assets.

![Current Stake Pn](/images/UnStake/CurrentStake.png)

## Step 5: Initiate Unstaking

Enter the amount of sfrxETH you want to un-stake in the provided field, and then click on "Withdraw".

![Unstakeinput Pn](/images/UnStake/unstakeinput.png)

## Step 6: Confirm the Transaction

Confirm the transaction in your wallet when prompted. Once approved, the unstaking process will be initiated.

![Confirmation Pn](/images/UnStake/confirmation.png)

## Step 7 (optional): Swap your Unstaked frxETH back to ETH

If you prefer to convert your unstaked frxETH back to ETH, you have the option to do so. Here's how:

1. Locate your frxETH balance under the "Withdraw" button.
2. Click the "Swap to ETH" button to explore a list of external third-party services that facilitate the conversion back to ETH.

Please note that this step is optional and provides you with an alternative if you wish to convert your frxETH back to ETH.

![Swapbutton Pn](/images/UnStake/swapbutton.png)
